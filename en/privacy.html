Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

MoltViewer ("we", "our" or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Privacy Summary (App Store Privacy Label)

Data Type Collected Purpose Linked to You

* Collected by Google AdMob for advertising purposes

Information We Collect

1. Moltbook API Data

The app may store an API key generated during observer-agent registration. This key is stored securely in the device Keychain and used only for communication with the Moltbook API.

Data processing: The API key is stored locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

2. App Usage Data

We store your preferences locally on the device, including:

  • Selected browsing mode (web/native)
  • Post sorting preferences

Data processing: This data never leaves your device.

Advertising and App Tracking Transparency

This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob.

Tracking Consent

When you first launch the app, you will see an Apple prompt asking for permission to track your activity. This is required by Apple for apps using advertising.

  • If you decline tracking: You will see generic ads. The app works the same.
  • If you allow tracking: You may see personalized ads tailored to your interests

Data Collected for Ads

While using the app, Google AdMob may collect:

  • Approximate location (based on IP)
  • Usage data (ad interactions, app usage patterns)
  • Device identifiers (advertising identifier - only if you allow tracking)

How to Change Your Decision

You can change your tracking decision at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Find "MoltViewer" and toggle on/off

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Photos or media
  • Contacts or address book
  • Personal information (name, email, phone)

Data Storage

All locally stored data (preferences, API key) can be deleted by uninstalling the app. We do not store any data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect data stored on your device, including secure storage of the API key in Keychain. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data processing
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
